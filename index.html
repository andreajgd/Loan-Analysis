‚úß What's Inside?

This project understands the loan performance, and is focused on two main areas:

  • 1. The Big Picture Metrics: I analyzed several key performance indicators (KPIs), such as Total Loan Applications, Total Funded Amount, Average Interest Rate, and Debt-to-Income Ratio (DTI).
  • 2. The Good, the Bad, and the Data: A major part of this project is a direct comparison of "good" loans versus "bad" loans. By comparing key metrics, I gained valuable insights into the factors that influence a loan's success or failure.
